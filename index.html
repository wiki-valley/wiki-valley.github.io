Welcome to WikiValley, the best website for beginners.

Stardew Valley is an endless country life RPG! You inherited your grandfather's old farm in Dewdale. Equipped with second-hand tools and a few coins, you'll start your new life. Can you learn to live off the land and turn these wildly overgrown fields into a prosperous home? It will not be easy. Since Joja Corporation came to town, the old ways of life have all but disappeared. The Community Center, once the most visited place in town, now lies in ruins. But the valley seems full of opportunity. With a little dedication, you could be the person to restore Dew Valley to greatness!
